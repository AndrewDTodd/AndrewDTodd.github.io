Sword of Apris - Feather Damascus Longsword

This is a model of a longsword I actually made for a D&D campaign. I have dubbed it the Sword of Apris. It features feather damascus, and a three-metal wire wrap on the hilt.

Modeled and rendered in blender, using the real time Eevee rendering engine.

Texturing was done in Substance Painter.
