Autastic Education - ABA therapy application

The Autastic project consists of two primary applications. This is the ABA therapy supplement app meant to be used by autistic children. I have been the director and technical lead on the project, coordinating efforts across the team. As well as my managerial role in this project, I have worked on individual levels and technologies used across the project.

This application gamifies and digitizes a selection of ABA therapy activities such that a child and their family can utilize these therapies at home each day, and at a dynamic pace appropriate to that child.

The Autastic project is an ongoing project. As such the images here are from a development build and do not necessarily reflect the finished product.

Wilderness Survival - sandbox survival simulator

Wilderness Survival is an Open World survival game the I built for the national FBLA competition my senior year of High School. Having been built to satisfy certain FBLA competition requirements - it was supposed to have an educational aspect - the game features survival tips as you play.

The game was built using Unity, as such game code was developed in c#. Some of the assets were obtained on the Unity marketplace, those created from scratch by me were modeled and textured in blender.

Unfortunately I have lost the source files for this project over the years. I was a lot less careful about backing things up in the past. However, I did at least keep a windows build of the game. It can be obtained in the link below if you are interested.
