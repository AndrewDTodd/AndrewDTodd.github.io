Sword of Apris - feather damascus longsword

This is a model of a longsword I actually made for a DnD campaign. I have dubbed it the Sword of Apris. It features feather damascus, and a three-metal wire wrap on the hilt.

Modeled and rendered in blender, using the real time Eevee rendering engine.

Texturing was done in Substance Painter.

Plasma Big Iron

Another concept of my own creation, a .44 magnum Big Iron.

Made complete by a 23rd century conversion from conventional .44 mag shells to nuclear powered hypersonic plasma rounds. Conveniently facilitated by a simple conversion from the stock cylinder to the two part plasma cartridge cylinder and electronic firing pin manifold. The electronic firing pins allow for circuit completion within the plasma cartridges nuclear fuel cell upon hammer strike. The cell's thermal overload causes a slug of reactive material to begin melting as it is propelled down the barrel to over 1830 m/s (6000+ ft/s) and a maximum temperature of 10000℃ (~18000℉).

Modeled and rendered in blender using the Eevee rendering engine.

Github Repo for the Plasma Revolver model (no textures included)

Bullpup Battle Rifle

This is one of my favorite firearm models I have created. Unfortunately I haven't been able to really finish it yet, so what you are seeing is a work in progress, but I think its far enough along to appreciate.

Chambered in 7.62x39 this bullpup rifle is the mechanical and stylistic mashup of something between an Ak-47 and an IWI Tavor. The operating system is long-stroke gas piston, heavily inspired by the Ak-47 and RPK platforms. The body of the firearm however takes its inspiration from the ergonomics and layout of the Tavor, as well as a bit of Ar-15 rail modularity for good measure.

Modeled and rendered in blender using the Eevee rendering engine.

Github Repo for the Bullpup Battle Rifle project (model state as shown, no textures included)
